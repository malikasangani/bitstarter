Make My Meals is a service that delivers gourmet home cooked meals to your doorstep!

All you need to do is enter your zip code and order online. Our service will connect you up with a local chef in your area that prepares & delivers home cooked meals!

We take care of all your special dietary needs. If you have any dietary restrictions such as sugar free, low salt, low fat, or anything else, we take care of it for you!

You can order food online or download our Android/iphone app makemymeals

Select from our delicious menu and then place an order. You can also select our weekly or monthly packages where you sign up for meals for as many days or weeks of a month. Leave it to the experts from there!

Please help us serve you and your fellow customers better by writing reviews for the food & service we provide. If you would like to make meals for our customers, please Email Me your information.

FAQ

Who Am I & Why this service?
As a geeky Web Technologist & crazy food lover, I combined my two passions to give you this special service. I am a trained Nutritionist too, so rest assure your meals are prepared with great care, love, and healthy ingredients. For those of you who are local to the San Francisco Bay Area, come join the cooking lessons we offer!
Our Mission & Vision
Our goal is to provide all our customers delicious and healthy meals. We understand you lead busy lives, may be elderly, or just may not have the inclination to prepare home cooked meals. We will connect you to local chefs in your area.

Currently, our roadmap begins with a few metropolitan cities. We will add more cities as we get more local folks to join our program. We hope you find our service and pricing competitive and affordable.
To all local cooks out there, come join our service, and we will connect you to a community who actively look forward to using your service.
